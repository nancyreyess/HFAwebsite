The Health for All Partnership fuses student-led clinics and community alliances to deliver integrated health services to underserved populations. Our distinct clinic model skillfully bridges healthcare disparities while fostering practical learning opportunities.

Our Community Partners

St. Francis Table

St. Francis Table, an integral part of Capuchin Outreach in Toronto's Parkdale, serves up to 250 meals six days a week. This initiative combats hunger and nurtures dignity, offering more than meals by fostering community resilience and unity.

Mennonite New Life Centre of Toronto

As a community-based settlement agency, the Mennonite New Life Centre of Toronto is a place of welcome, friendship and community, where newcomers and neighbours gather to support each other, learn from each other, and take action together for a more just and compassionate society.

Canadian Mental Health Association - Peel Dufferin & Peel Family Shelter

CMHA Peel champions good mental health for everyone and supports the full participation of those with mental illness and addictions in the life of the community.
